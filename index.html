About Me

I am a talented software developer with 3 years of experience at Hewlett Packard Enterprise in Austin TX with an in-depth understanding of the software development lifecyle and a growing knowledge of the CI/CD pipeline. My team is responsible for supporting and implementing IT solutions to HPE employees worldwide utilizing the latest technologies and incorporating new methodologies that are quickly gaining popularity in the industry such as dev-ops, chat-ops and agile project management.

With this team I have exposed myself to a large variety of tools and resources allowing me to increase my knowledge in the field and really discover my value as a developer. During my tenure at HPE I have had the chance to work with multiple coding languages like Java, C#, Python, Bash and html. I have also been able to master database manipulation with MSSQL and administration of the Linux infrastructure as well as gain a detailed understanding of IT for business operations.

Right now I am staying relevant by learning as much as I can and improving my skills as an IT Professional and Software Developer.





How I Enjoy Working

I mostly enjoy working as part of a hardworking, cooperative team but through my position at Hewlett Packard I quickly learned how to branch out and become self sufficient when working on my own. I learned the importance of what it means to be more hands on and to always keep learning new things for myself in order to stay relevant and be able to contribute to the overall production value of the company.
